About Me

Ion Eremia

I'm a triple-certified professional covering IT Support, Handyman services, and Passive Fire Protection — based in Borehamwood, serving businesses across London and surrounding areas since 2020.

On the IT side, I hold CompTIA A+, Microsoft Azure Administrator Associate, Cisco CCENT, and a Level 2 Cybersecurity qualification. I've deployed and configured office networks, set up VPNs for remote workforces, and provided hands-on hardware and software support for businesses in Greater London.

On the construction and fire safety side, I spent 5+ years at Lesterose Builders Ltd as a Mastic Applicator and Passive Fire Protection Operative — holding NVQ Level 2 in both Joint Sealant Application and Passive Fire Protection, plus SSSTS (CITB), IPAF, and Fire Warden certification.

I believe reliability is the most undervalued quality in a contractor. I show up. I finish the job. I communicate clearly. That's the FixIT Pro promise.
